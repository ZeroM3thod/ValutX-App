FAQ
Common Questions
How do I deposit funds?
Send USDT (TRC-20) to your assigned wallet address. Deposits are credited within 30 minutes after network confirmation. Minimum deposit is $100 USDT.
When can I withdraw?
Withdrawals are available after the 30-day security lock period expires. During an active season, your invested funds remain locked until season close.
How are referral commissions paid?
You earn 15% of every profit your referred investors make. Commissions are automatically credited to your wallet balance at season close.
What is a Season?
A Season is a 90-day managed investment pool. Each season has a defined ROI range. Your funds are actively managed during this period.
